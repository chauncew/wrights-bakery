About Us

When in Chicago, don't miss a trip to Wright's Bakery. Residents consider Wright's, with its rustic outdoor cafe and beautiful view of the skyline and to be part of the social night life. A place to meet people, bump into old friends, or sit quietly and enjoy the view. Known since 1981 for great bagels, croissants, rolls, rugulah, danish adn butter cookies. Wright's includes a coffee bar and full lunch menu. Experience Chicago with Wright's.
